Call your care provider when contractions reach the 5-1-1 pattern: 5 minutes apart (start to start), lasting at least 1 minute, consistently for 1 hour. Some providers say 4-1-1 — confirm with yours in advance. When in doubt, call.

Irregular → 5–20 min apart
30–60 sec long. Stay home. Rest, eat, walk. Can still talk through them.
Active Labor
3–5 min apart
45–60 sec long. Head to hospital. Cannot talk through contractions.
Transition
2–3 min apart
60–90 sec long. Most intense. Shortest phase. Birth is close. Stay present.
