When Labor Begins — Partner's Checklist
Start timing contractionsApp or paper sheet — start to start, and duration
Stay calm — early labor can be long
Keep environment relaxed, not urgent
Eat and drink — both of you
Call care provider when contractions are 5-1-15 min apart, 1 min long, for 1 hour
Grab hospital bag and birth preferences
Notify the people on your list
Put phone away once active labor beginsYour presence is the job now
5-1-1 Rule
For first-time parents: head to the hospital when contractions are 5 minutes apart, lasting 1 minute, for at least 1 hour. Ask your care provider if their guidance differs — some say 4-1-1. When in doubt, call.
